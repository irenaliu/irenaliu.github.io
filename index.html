Projects

OutToLunch Mobile App

OutToLunch is a mobile application I made as my final course project in the 10-week android development course at NAIT. The idea behind it is that the app could help indecisive people (like me) decide what kind of food to eat around their area. This app will first choose a random cuisine from a predetermined list of cuisines. Once a cuisine is selected, the app will return the places that are still open at the time of the query.
