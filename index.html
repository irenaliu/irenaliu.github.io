About

Hello! My name is Irena. I'm a Software Developer at Blendable, where I get to design and enhance complete technical solutions — from user interfaces to app infrastructure.

My coding journey started in childhood, spending hours dabbling in customizing profile pages on Neopets and later Calgary Planet/Nexopia. Years later, I rediscovered that passion and graduated from the Digital Media & IT program at the Northern Alberta Institute of Technology, majoring in Computer Software Development.

Today, I enjoy working with data, delivering impactful features, and writing clean, maintainable code, while ensuring applications look and feel great. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and refine my skills.

Beyond tech, I care deeply about sustainable living and hope to use my skills to support businesses and industries that promote environmental sustainability in the future.
